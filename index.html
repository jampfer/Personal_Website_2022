About Me

I am 22 years old and will be graduating from NKU in the spring of 2023. Throughout my time at NKU, I have accomplished many things. Together, we facilitated 17 orientation programs for first time freshmen, in total serving over 1,000 incoming students. Currently, I am the social media and communications intern for the Office of Admissions at NKU where I create and post content that showcases the best parts of the University. Upon graduation, I plan to attend grad school at NKU in the fall where I will study Business Informatics with a focus in Analytics.
