Hobbies and Favorite Activities

I have a variety of different hobbies and favorite activities. Let's check them out!

Playing the drums

I love playing the drums. I got my first drumset when I was a senior in high school and have been playing ever since. After moving into an apartment, I quickly discovered that drumsets become much louder when played in the garage. Because of this, I got an electric kit, which is much quieter and allows for headphones. While it does not come close to the sensation of playing real drums, I enjoy discovering the various different pre-loaded sounds and music clips stored in the instrument's memory.
